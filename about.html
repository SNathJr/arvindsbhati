About

Hi, I'm Arvind Singh Bhati. I am a smartphone photography enthusiast and a student at Integrated Science Education and Research Center, Visva-Bharati. Nearly everyday I catch my cycle in the evening and set out for a photography adventure nearby where I live.

I have taken part in many photography contests such as the Wikimedia contest, NatGeo photography contest by National Geographic India and INQUIVESTA-16 by IISER, Kolkata. I also work as a photographer and camera person for our design group Team CodeOn. When I am not taking photos I enjoy expercising, dancing, tasting new cuisines and Cycling.
